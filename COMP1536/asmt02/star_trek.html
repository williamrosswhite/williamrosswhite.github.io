Welcome to my Star Trek Website!



This website is dedicated to my own personal likes and dislikes in the television universe of Star Trek. The currently produced show "Star Trek: Discovery, while it looks promising so far, is not covered since most of these shows get better half way through their run, and I don't feel it fair to judge it against the other until it can be appreciated in a proper context once it's full run is complete. That leaves TOS, TNG, DS9, Voy, and Ent.


Star Trek Shows
Shows The Good The Bad
Star Trek: The Original Series Truest to Roddenberry's original vision and began it all. Primordial special effects and campy acting.
Star Trek: The Next Generation Roddenberry created, improved by others. Showed humanity at its best. ...it ended.
Star Trek: Deep Space Nine Counterpoint to TNG, explored the darkness of humanity. Concept was a shameless rip off of Babylon 5
Star Trek: Voyager Blended TNG and DS9, showed utopian ideals stressed by extreme challenge. Kes and the Kayzon, but both were limited to early seasons.
